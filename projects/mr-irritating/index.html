Challange

Love it or hate it, the fact is people do judge a book by its cover.

Designing novel cover is both fun and challenging. Because the interiors of most novels are unadorned continuous typography I think you have to take a lot of care to balance the elements of the page to get the best effect. Margins, line leading, choice of fonts get more critical since the aim of the designer is to enable the reader to be carried along by the author's story.

We were wanted something that directlt tell the brief about the romantic stoty. The novel should stand out and attracts passing by people when kept on shop. the colour, typography and character styling, their background story.

Love it or hate it, the fact is people do judge a book by its cover.

It was important for me to design a cover that narrates the story in just a blink of an eye and

This is what I was briefed

Anant is an ordinary shy boy hailing from Varanasi. He has just come to Lucknow to pursue his engineering and finds everything very different to him. He never thought that this place would hold such an important place in his life. While in his first semester, his eyes capture a girl who turned his world upside down. He’s gripped by love at first sight and begins to follow his desire for his beloved Aashi, blindly. Every day, there is a round table discussion on love and it’s side effects. His enthusiastic friends help him with their expert advices. Everything is very pleasant in the beginning. With the passage of days, he manages to win her friendship but inside he was madly in love with her. One of his close friends, Kavya happens to help him build his confidence and in the process, she falls for him. After few days, he finds out that the girl he loved, already had a boyfriend and considers Anant a bit irritating. This breaks him. Anant actually loved her enough to let her go. Kavya is senior to him, frank, bold and pure hearted girl, who had always been at Anant’s backend. Anant resists forgetting Aashi, while Kavya is always ready to move mountains for him. Thus, Anant struggles between love,friendship and career. Mr. Irritating is the journey of the three, in pursuit of their true love.

Is it so hard to win over your first love? Will life never stop playing strange games? Can love be found from unexpected places?

Well, somethings are better left unsaid. Love has its own way of troubling people. It is capable of making you and breaking you. Presenting to you a roller coaster ride with amazing twists and turns. Mr. Irritating shall not irritate you!

Kavya

The best friend of Mr.Irritating. She is modern,bold girl.A guitarist, a basketball player and loves to wonder on her scooty.She hangs out with boys,unlike other girls who chit chat,much to dislike of Anant .As the story progresses,she become more than a friend for him ,with her unconditional support.However, the feelings come to notice off to late.

Anant

Anant, is a shy guy hailing from Banaras who comes down to Lucknow to pursue engineering. He finds his love on the stairs of collage one fine day and spends his time on getting to her. He finds love and friendship, then looses both, embracing disillusionment!

Aashi

Aashi is deeply loved by mr.Irritating. She is a sweet and bubbly girl, with limited friends. She has a reserved nature and looks beautiful in indian Attires. As Anant confesses his love, she accepts it, becoming his strength through the thick and thin of collage life. However, in the end misunderstandings develop and she leaves him without a word.
